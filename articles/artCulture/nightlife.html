Late-night activity in Santa Fe tends to wrap up earlier than your average city, but bartenders are still waiting to make your favorite adult beverage—or theirs:  

Agave Lounge in the Eldorado Hotel
309 W San Francisco St., 995-4530
Leather couches, good tequila, champagne and food too.

Del Charro Saloon
101 W Alameda St., 954-0320
Locals unwind in front of walk-out windows with house margs here.

Secreto Bar in the Hotel St. Francis
210 Don Gaspar Ave., 983-5700
Signature cocktails include the Spicy Secreto: rum with a chile ring.

Cowgirl BBQ
319 S. Guadalupe, 988-4227
Bustling bar with frequent live music.

Evangelo’s Cocktail Lounge
200 W San Francisco St.,
Raise a glass of ouzo to WWII veteran Evangelo Klonis, then drink cheap beer.

El Farol
808 Canyon Road, 983-9912
Lots of live music, plus dancing and Spanish wine.

Pranzo Italian Grille
540 Montezuma Ave., 984-2645
Wine by the glass that comes in regular and gigante.
